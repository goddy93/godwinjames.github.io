About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Donald Danforth Plant Science Center, USA. My research explores plant-pathogen interactions and antifungal mechanisms of plant peptides such as NCR13. Using biochemical, molecular, and structural biology approaches, I aim to uncover how plant-derived peptides modulate fungal physiology and activate immune signaling.

Research Interests

  • Structure–activity relationship of antimicrobial peptides
  • Mechanisms of peptide-mediated fungal inhibition
  • Plant immunity and peptide–receptor signaling
  • Functional genomics and protein biochemistry
